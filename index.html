About MoldTrip

MoldTrip is your gateway to discovering the hidden gems of Moldova. We specialize in organizing unique excursions and trips to the most breathtaking landmarks of our country. From ancient fortresses to serene nature reserves, we provide carefully curated experiences that blend history, nature, and culture. Whether you're an avid explorer or a history enthusiast, our tours offer an unforgettable journey through Moldova's rich heritage.

Our team consists of passionate local guides who know every corner of the region. We handle all the logistics — transportation, meals, and guided storytelling — so you can immerse yourself in the beauty of each destination without worrying about the details. Each trip is designed to offer an authentic taste of Moldova, with opportunities to try local wines, traditional dishes, and engage with local artisans.

Why choose MoldTrip? We prioritize personalized experiences, small group sizes, and flexible itineraries to match your interests. Our goal is not just to show you places but to create memories that last a lifetime. Join us, and let's embark on a journey to explore Moldova like never before!
